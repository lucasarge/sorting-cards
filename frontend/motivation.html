Instructions:


You’ll see a set of Motivational Driver cards.

Each card describes something that can feed a person’s energy, interest, or motivation.

There’s no right way to do this.

Go with what feels most true for you.


Step 1 — Read & Notice

Read through the cards one by one.

As you do, notice:

  • which cards feel energising or meaningful
  • which cards feel neutral
  • which cards feel draining or effortful

You don’t need to analyse why.

Just notice your reaction.


Step 2 — Choose Your Top Four

From the full set, choose four drivers that matter most to you overall.

Think whole of life, not just work.

These are the drivers that:

  • help you feel engaged
  • make things feel more sustainable
  • matter even when life gets busy

Step 3 — Choose One Main Driver

From your four, choose one main driver.

This is the driver that feels:

  • most important right now, or
  • hardest to do without

You don’t need to justify this choice.


Take Your Time

If you want to pause partway through, that’s fine.

If you want to come back later, that’s fine too.

When you’re ready, move on to the reflection.
